Holi Wishes, Just For You

🌈

May Colors Find You

Even on your greyest days, may the colors of Holi remind you that life is outrageously beautiful — and so are you.

💙

New Chapter Energy

Holi washes away the old. May this mark the beginning of something wonderful — a dream, a friendship, a fresh start.

🌸

Joy That Sticks

Just like gulal on a white kurta — may joy stick to you this year and refuse to wash off no matter what life throws. 😄

🔥

Burn the Bad Stuff

Holika burns tonight. May everything that no longer serves you — stress, grudges, self-doubt — go with it. 🕯️

🎊

Laugh Until You Cry

May you have at least one moment today where you laugh so hard your stomach hurts and colors are flying everywhere. 😂

✨

You're Someone's Color

To someone out there, you are the color that makes their world brighter. Never forget that. Happy Holi, wonderful human. 💛
